Privacy Policy

brandonfrankspottery.com

What Information Is Collected

This site collects only the information you voluntarily submit through the contact form: your name, email address, and message. No other personal data is collected. There are no accounts, no logins, and no tracking tied to your identity.

How Your Information Is Used

Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry. Your name and email are never sold, rented, shared with third parties for marketing purposes, or used for any purpose beyond the conversation you initiated.

Third-Party Services

Formspree — Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree, a third-party form handling service. When you submit the contact form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to this site's email address. Formspree's own privacy policy governs how they handle that data in transit.

Pinterest — This site uses Pinterest's domain verification tag. Pinterest may collect limited data in accordance with their privacy policy if you interact with Pinterest features on or off this site.

Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address as part of standard request handling. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Cookies and Analytics

This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Basic analytics may be collected by GitHub Pages (the hosting platform) as part of standard server logging. No behavioral tracking, remarketing pixels, or advertising cookies are used on this site.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and are not stored in any database on this site. Formspree's retention policies apply to data processed through their service.

Your Rights

You may request that any information you submitted through the contact form be deleted by emailing brandonfrankspottery@gmail.com. This site does not collect enough data to warrant formal data subject access request procedures, but any reasonable request will be honored promptly.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to brandonfrankspottery@gmail.com.
